Who this program is for

This program is built for professionals with a few years of work experience who want a recognised MBA without leaving their job. Cohorts are typically a mix of engineers, managers and business owners.

What you'll study

The program covers core management disciplines in the first year, followed by electives and a capstone project in the second.

  • Financial & Managerial Accounting
  • Marketing Management
  • Operations & Strategy
  • Organizational Behaviour
  • Capstone Project
