IIT Bombay and IIT Roorkee: Two Different Takes on Tech Leadership

Both institutes run technology-leadership certifications through Jaro Education, but they're built for different points in a career — not competing versions of the same thing.

IIT Bombay and IIT Roorkee are both premier technology institutes running executive certifications through Jaro Education, and it's easy to assume their programs overlap more than they actually do.

IIT Bombay: leadership-first

IIT Bombay's Chief Technology Officer Programme is explicitly built for senior technology leaders — an 8-month program aimed at people already in or moving into CTO-adjacent roles, where the emphasis is on connecting technology strategy to business outcomes rather than teaching new technical skills from scratch. IIT Bombay also runs a Sustainability Leadership and Practice certificate, a one-year program aimed at a different but related executive audience.

IIT Roorkee: hands-on and dual-track

IIT Roorkee's Continuing Education Centre (CEC) takes a different approach with two programs: a PG Certificate in Applied Data Science & AI aimed at individual contributors moving into ML and analytics roles, and a Chief Technology & AI Officer PG Certificate aimed at senior leaders — covering both the practitioner and executive ends of the spectrum within one institute.

How to actually choose

If you're already leading a technology function and need the strategic and business-communication layer, IIT Bombay's CTO Programme is the more direct fit. If you're earlier in a technical career and want a credentialed on-ramp into applied AI/ML work, IIT Roorkee's Data Science & AI certificate is built for that stage — with its own CTO-track certificate available later once you're ready for the leadership jump.
