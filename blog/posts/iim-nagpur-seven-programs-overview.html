IIM Nagpur Through Jaro: Seven Programs, One Partnership

IIM Nagpur's partnership with Jaro Education, launched in 2021, has grown from four founding programs into seven — making it one of the most active single-institute relationships in Jaro's portfolio.

When IIM Nagpur and Jaro Education announced their collaboration in 2021, the institute's director Dr. Bhimaraya Metri described the goal plainly: curate blended, techno-functional PG certificate programs across management specializations that address real industry skill gaps.

The original four

The 2021 launch covered Data Science, Business Management in IT, FinTech, and Digital Strategy & Marketing Analytics — each a 9-to-12-month blended program combining online coursework with campus immersion at IIM Nagpur.

What's been added since

The catalog has since expanded to include a Supply Chain Operations & Analytics certificate and a Cyber Security Management & Data Science program, alongside the institute's flagship offering: the 2-year Blended MBA for Working Professionals, IIM Nagpur's answer to full-time MBA equivalence without a career pause.

A consistent structure

Across all seven programs, the pattern holds: blended delivery (online coursework plus a campus immersion component), PG Certificate plus IIM Nagpur Executive Alumni status on completion, and a functional focus tied to specific industry roles rather than generic management theory.

For a mid-second-generation IIM — established in 2015 — this breadth of program design is a notable bet on the blended-executive-education format specifically, rather than trying to replicate a traditional MBA online.
