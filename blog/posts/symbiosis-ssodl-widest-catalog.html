Symbiosis SSODL: The Widest Degree Catalog in Jaro's DLP Network

Most of Jaro's DLP university partners offer one flagship degree — usually an MBA. Symbiosis SSODL is the exception, running six distinct UG and PG programs through the same partnership.

Symbiosis School for Online and Digital Learning (SSODL) is the online arm of Symbiosis International (Deemed University), an institution with over five decades of history dating back to 1971. That legacy shows up in how broad SSODL's online catalog is compared to peer DLP universities.

Six degrees, not one

Through Jaro Education, SSODL offers an Online MBA, Online BBA, Online BCA, an MSc in Data Science, an MA in Mass Communication, and an MSc in Economics — spanning business, technology, humanities, and social science in a single university partnership.

Why the spread matters

For a prospective learner, this breadth means SSODL is worth considering even outside the MBA conversation — a working professional wanting a data science postgraduate degree, for instance, has an SSODL option that most other DLP partners simply don't offer.

Recognition consistency

All six programs share the same accreditation backbone: recognized by the UGC and approved by AICTE, taught by faculty drawn from Symbiosis's network of institutes, and delivered fully online with no mandatory campus visit — a structural consistency that's easier to maintain across a narrower catalog, which makes SSODL's breadth more notable.
