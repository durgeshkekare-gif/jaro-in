IIT Roorkee's Applied Data Science & AI Certificate: Curriculum Breakdown

Not every "data science certificate" covers the same ground. Here's what's specific about IIT Roorkee's version, delivered over 6-8 months through Jaro Education.

IIT Roorkee's Continuing Education Centre (CEC) runs the PG Certificate in Applied Data Science & AI as a 6-8 month program aimed at professionals moving into or formalising skills in machine learning and applied analytics roles.

Who it's built for

The program targets two overlapping groups: engineers and IT professionals looking to pivot into data science roles, and analysts who've picked up ML skills informally and want a credentialed, structured foundation to back it up.

The technical core

Expect coverage of statistical foundations, Python-based tooling, machine learning and deep learning fundamentals, and data visualization — with a capstone project that typically anchors a portfolio piece for job applications or internal role changes.

How it compares to IIT Roorkee's other CEC offering

IIT Roorkee also runs a Chief Technology & AI Officer PG Certificate through the same CEC channel — a more senior, leadership-oriented program. The Applied Data Science & AI certificate is the more hands-on, individual-contributor-level counterpart.
