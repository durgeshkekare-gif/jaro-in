Amrita Online: NAAC A++ Meets Flexible MBA Specializations

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham holds NAAC A++ accreditation and Institution of Eminence status — but the more practical detail for prospective MBA students is how many specialization tracks it now runs through Jaro Education.

Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham is a multi-campus university recognized globally for research impact alongside academic quality, and the Online MBA delivered via Amrita Online carries the same UGC-entitled status as the on-campus equivalent.

What NAAC A++ actually means

NAAC A++ is the highest accreditation grade India's National Assessment and Accreditation Council awards — it reflects institutional quality across teaching, research, and governance, evaluated on a points scale. It's a credible signal, though it applies to the university as a whole rather than certifying any single online program's outcomes.

Beyond the base MBA

Through Jaro, Amrita now offers dedicated MBA specialization tracks — including an MBA in FinTech and an MBA in ESG — alongside the general Online MBA, which itself covers Analytics, AI, Marketing, Finance, Operations, and HR as elective tracks within a four-semester structure.

The flexibility trade-off

All Amrita Online MBA formats share a minimum 2-year, maximum 4-year completion window — useful for learners whose work schedules are unpredictable, though it means the degree timeline is genuinely open-ended rather than a fixed 2-year commitment.
