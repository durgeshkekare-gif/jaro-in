Inside the IIM Nagpur Blended MBA: What the Campus Immersion Involves

"Blended" is doing a lot of work in the program name. Here's what the actual split between online coursework and on-campus time looks like for IIM Nagpur's 2-year Blended MBA.

The Blended MBA for Working Professionals is IIM Nagpur's flagship offering through Jaro Education — a 2-year program built specifically so participants don't need to pause full-time employment to complete it.

The structure

The bulk of coursework happens through live online sessions and recorded lectures, scheduled around working hours. Campus immersion modules bring cohorts to IIM Nagpur periodically for intensive, in-person components — case discussions, peer networking, and faculty interaction that's harder to replicate purely online.

Who actually enrolls

Cohorts tend to be a mix of engineers, mid-level managers, and small business owners — people with a few years of work experience who want the credential and the network without the opportunity cost of a full-time program.

What to weigh before applying

Campus immersion is typically a mandatory component (not optional), so factor in travel and time-off planning around those specific windows. It's a meaningfully different commitment than a fully remote certificate — closer to a hybrid degree than an online course.
