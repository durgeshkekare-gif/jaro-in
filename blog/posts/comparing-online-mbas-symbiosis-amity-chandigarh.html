Comparing Online MBAs: Symbiosis vs. Amity vs. Chandigarh University

When all three options carry the same UGC/AICTE approval and roughly the same 2-year format, the real differences come down to specialization depth and institutional focus.

Symbiosis SSODL, Amity University Online, and Chandigarh University Online are three of the most commonly compared Online MBA options in Jaro's DLP network — all UGC-entitled, all roughly 2 years, all built for working professionals.

Symbiosis SSODL

Backed by Symbiosis International's 50+ year academic reputation, and notable for sitting inside a wider degree catalog (BBA, BCA, MSc Data Science, and more) rather than being a standalone MBA product.

Amity University Online

Runs a globally accredited MBA with 12 specialization areas and an AI-driven personalised learning platform — the widest specialization spread of the three, spanning finance, marketing, HR, operations, and international business.

Chandigarh University Online

Offers dual specialisation options within a single MBA, UGC-entitled and AICTE-approved, with a curriculum built specifically around industry-alignment rather than breadth of electives.

How to actually choose

If specialization variety matters most, Amity's 12 tracks stand out. If you want the MBA alongside optionality for other degrees under one university relationship, Symbiosis SSODL's broader catalog is the more flexible bet. Chandigarh University suits someone who's already decided on a narrower dual-specialisation path.
